Getting into town

The ceremony will be on Sat 13 October, 2018. We suggest arriving Friday and leaving Sunday. Nederland ("Ned" to the cool kids) is in the mountains, just the other side of Boulder. For most people, it will be easiest to fly into Denver airport. If you're renting a car, directions to Nederland are "easy".

If you want to take public transportation from the airport, there are several good options (with solid schedules even on Sunday) to get to us:

  • There is a bus straight from the airport to Boulder
  • If you want to go to downtown Denver first for something, you can take the A-line train into Denver, and then an FF1 bus into Boulder later.
  • If you are arriving to Denver by train (Amtrak's California Zephyr stops in Denver) you'll also want to take the FF1 into Boulder, although you're probably so dedicated to public transportation that you already knew that.
  • Once you're in Boulder, there is an N bus that runs straight to Nederland.

    • As we get a little closer to the actual date, we'll coordinate with local people and people renting cars to try to make it as painless as possible. Most likely someone can give you a ride from the airport, or pick you up from Denver or Boulder.

    Gifts

    Please don't bring us gifts. We really just want to hang out and celebrate, and we already have most of the things we need. We aren't registering anywhere. If you started hand weaving us a pair of traditional Nootka whaler basket hats 4 years ago and can't think of what else to do with them, okay, bring 'em, but seriously, there's no obligation of any kind.

    Pictures

    I'm sure you'll want to pull your phones out and take pictures during the ceremony, but please try to keep it to a minimum. We'd love it if you could just join us and enjoy yourselves in the moment.

    Attire

    We're aiming to have things be pretty casual. You could get away with jeans, or just something clean looking, but probably don't want a tie, and a suit is out of the question. If you can't decide between two levels of formality, just pick whichever one you feel more comfortable in, I'm sure nobody will mind.

    Weather

    We're hoping the weather will be nice out, but who knows! In October, there's a possibility of sunshine or snow. Or RAIN - on your wedding day; wouldn't that be ironic?

    Activities

    There's a hot tub, so bring a bathing suit if that sounds fun. If you want to hike or run, there should be plenty of places to scratch that itch.
