Getting into town

The ceremony will be on Sat 13 October, 2018. We suggest arriving Friday and leaving Sunday. Nederland is in the mountains, just the other side of Boulder. For most people, it will be easiest to fly into Denver airport, then take the train to downtown, and the bus to Boulder, or just take the bus straight there from the airport. If you're renting a car, directions to Nederland are "easy". As we get a little closer to the actual date, we'll coordinate with local people and people renting cars to try to make it as painless as possible.

Other Notes

Important things to know

Gifts

Please don't bring us gifts. We really just want to hang out and celebrate, and we already have most of the things we need. We aren't registering anywhere. If you started hand weaving us a pair of traditional Hupa basket hats 4 years ago and can't think of what else to do with them, okay, bring 'em, but seriously, there's no obligation of any kind.

Pictures

I'm sure you'll want to pull your phones out and take pictures during the ceremony, but please try to keep it to a minimum. We'd love it if you could just join us and enjoy yourselves in the moment.

Attire

We're aiming to have things be pretty casual. You could get away with jeans, or just something clean, but probably don't want a tie, and a suit is out of the question. If you're can't decide between two levels of formality, just pick whichever one you feel more comfortable in, I'm sure nobody will mind.

Weather

We're hoping the weather will be nice out, but who knows! In October, there's a possibility of sunshine or snow. Or RAIN - on your wedding day; wouldn't that be ironic?

Activities

There's a hot tub, so bring a bathing suit if that sounds fun. If you want to hike or run, there should be plenty of places to scratch that itch.

Your Plans

Let us know if your plans change and you can't make it. We're only planning for a small group to join us, so we want to make sure we're planning correctly.
